I am an online retailer, so digital devices are very important. I keep my phone and computer charged so I can answer calls and emails. I also use my computer to develop my website.

Even though my retail shop is online, I also attend music festivals and guitar shows to sell my guitars. At these festivals, I use an extension cord to power different devices. One important device is an amplifier. Customers plug in an electric guitar to the amplifier to hear the sound it makes.

At outdoor music festivals and guitar shows, I use an extension cord to power a fan to keep the guitars cool. If they get too hot, they can be damaged.
