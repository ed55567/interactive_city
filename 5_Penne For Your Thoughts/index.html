Cooking equipment, such as a stove and oven, is essential to a food truck. Since the food truck is mobile, we need an easy and portable way to power the cooking equipment. Thatâ€™s why we use propane! Propane gas comes in a tank that connects to our cooking equipment. You may have also seen a propane tank on a camping stove or gas grill.





Without light, it would be hard to see inside the food truck. We use a generator to power our lights. Generators supply electricity when other power sources are not available.

At the food truck, we rely on electronic devices, such as a TV and a credit card machine. Why do we need a TV on a food truck? We use the TV screen to display the menu so customers know what food we offer. We use the credit card machine to process payments. This machine allows us to sell food to customers who do not carry cash.

Both the TV and the credit card machine are powered by electricity. This electricity comes from a generator.
